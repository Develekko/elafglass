Few Words About Us

Elaf For Glass and Decoration Company was established since 2018. We work in the field of Securite glass, where our business includes (Facades - Partitions & doors - shower cabinets - Mirrors - Handrails & Stairs - Spider facades - Glass Cladding - Glass floors) We have done a lot of distinguished projects in the commercial fields such as Malls, companies, offices, villages, Compounds and so on . We provide all operating requirements for projects with the highest degree of quality and efficiency, as well as specialized technicians at the highest level of technical skills, and our clients testify to that.
